About

I am a Researcher at Ho Chi Minh City Institute of Physics, Vietnam Academy of Science and Technology.

My research interests:

  • Smart window with optical modulation, heat-shielding, and solar-energy haversting.
  • Smart, responsive materials for soft-actuators and soft-robotics.
  • Environmental-friendly and electrical power-free smart actuators.
  • Carbon-based material (CNT or graphene) and polymer composites.
  • Aligned CNT sheets and yarn with guest functional materials for multi-functional devices.

    CNT sheet-paraffin composite

    Multi-functional smart windows

    Accomplishments

    • ---> Orientations: Further development of high-performance flexible and foldable smart window with optical modulation, heat-shielding, and solar-energy haversting.

    CNT and liquid crystal combination

    Novel liquid crystal devices

    Accomplishments
  • A single-layer CNT-sheet functions as an alignment layer and a transparent electrode with superior electro-thermo-optical performance.

    • ---> Orientations: Further development of high-performance flexible and foldable LC display devices.

    Current Applied Physics, 2016

    Paraffin infiltrated CNT yarn

    Smart actuators, artificial muscles

    Accomplishments
  • Large volume expansion of the solid to liquid phase change of the paraffin.
  • Radial expansion for the neat yarn proceeds to the axial contraction for the coiled yarn.
  • The coiling effect directly makes the radial change to the axial strain variation.

    • ---> Orientations: Further development of high-performance artificial muscles.

    Current Applied Physics, 2014

    rGO-FET

    Field effect transitor for pH Sensing

    Accomplishments
  • Reduced graphene oxide field-effect transistor (R-GO FET) with indium tin oxide (ITO) extended gate electrode was fabricated.
  • The ITO extended gate electrode was also used as the proton-sensing region.
  • The proton-sensing properties based on the R-GO FET transducer were analyzed.

    • ---> Orientations: The sensor has offered a potential for sensing of H+ in the electrolytes, and the sensing area can be modified further for various ions sensing applications with high stability, sensitivity, and selectivity.

    Bachelor thesis, 2009

    Mn Doped ZnS nanoparticles

    Photoluminescence studies for bio-image diagnostics

    Accomplishments
  • Research and synthesize Mn-doped ZnS nanoparticles.
  • XRD, UV-Vis, TEM and PL results showed that these particles have a potential for applying in bio-image diagnostics.

    • ---> Orientations: A greener and luminescent material as a great candidate for fluorescent labeling agent in bio-image diagnostics.
